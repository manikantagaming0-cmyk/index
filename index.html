About Me

I don't take pictures.
I find them.

I'm Mani — a freelance photographer with over eight years of experience chasing light across golden hours, bustling city streets, and intimate private moments.

My work lives at the intersection of documentary honesty and painterly composition. Whether I'm shooting a wedding, a portrait session, or an editorial for a brand, I believe the best photographs feel inevitable — like the camera simply confirmed what was already there.

I'm available for commissions worldwide, and I'm always excited to collaborate with people who care about their story being told well.
